    Basics of Poker:
    Royal Flush: Five cards of the same suit. Ranked consecutively from Ace to Ten.
    Straight Flush To begin, deal each player two cards face down. These are called the "hole cards". Over five rounds, more cards will be dealt face up in the middle of the table. The future face up cards are called "community cards." Each player will use them to mak ea five-card poker hand.
